Most Effective uses of BANANA for Face and Hair

By Maisha

Bananas are grown through out the year. Its's one of the best ingredient to use for brightening your skin. Ripe Bananas are rich in Vitamin C and B6. It helps to improve the production of collagen in the skin that helps to maintain skin elasticity and suppleness. It also helps to get rid of wrinkle and acne. Bananas aren't only good for skin but hair too. It helps in promoting hair growth and keeps hair from drying out and getting damaged. It repairs damaged hair follicles and adds texture to it. I have included some of my fave banana mask for both hair and face. They are very effective and useful.

Banana Face Mask

METHOD-1

Item/ Ingredient

Rip Banana Yogurt Lemon

procedure

Mix 2 table spoon of banana with 1 table spoon of yogurt. And then add 1/2 tea spoon of lemon with it. Apply all over your face. Keep it for 20-30 minutes. Do it 2-3 times every week.

Benefits

Helps to reduce Wrinkles.


METHOD-2

Item/ Ingredient

Banana Honey Yogurt

procedure

Mash 2-3 pieces of banana. Mix 1 tea spoon of honey and 1 tea spoon of yogurt with it. Mix well and then apply it all over your face. keep it for 15-20 minutes. Repeat 2-3 times a week. If your acne reduces then apply 1-2 times a week.

Benefits

This will help you get bright and acne free face.


METHOD-3

Item/ Ingredient

Banana Honey Lemon

procedure

Mash 2-3 pieces of a rip Banana in a bowl. Then mix 1 tea spoon of honey and 1 tea spoon of lemon with it.Wash your face properly. Now apply the mask all over your face and neck precisely. Then keep it for 10-15 minutes. do this 1-2 times a week for 1 month.

Benefits

This mask will help you get brighter skin and get an Even skin tone. Honey will help in moisturizing your skin.


METHOD-4

Item/ Ingredient

Rip Banana Lemon

procedure

Mash 2-3 piece of a rip banana in a bowl. Now mix 1 tea spoon of lemon with it. Nicely rub it all over your face for 1 minute and then keep it for 10-15 minutes. Do this for 1-2 times a week. If you have dry skin you can keep it for a few less minute since lemon dries out skin too quick and some times causes irritations. You can do this once per week.

Benefits

This mask will help you get rid of dark spots and uneven skin. If your spots disappear then you can stop applying it.


METHOD-5

Item/ Ingredient

Banana Milk

procedure

Mash 1 rip banana and apply it all over your face and neck. If you want you can also add 2 tea spoon of milk with it and then apply. Keep it for 10-15 minutes. Wash your face with cold water. You can do this 2-3 times a week.

Benefits

This will make you skin fresh and fair.


Banana Hair Mask

METHOD-1

Item/ Ingredients

Rip Banana Olive Oil/ Coconut Oil Honey

Procedure

Mash 1/2 banana ( according to hair length). Add 2 tea spoon of olive oil and 1 tea spoon of coconut oil. Then add 2 tea spoon of honey with them. Increase the amount of Coconut Oil, Olive Oil and Honey according your hair length. Apply this mask 1-2 times a week.

Benefit

This will keep your hair conditioned,strong and healthy and will also repair your damaged hair.


METHOD-2

Item/ Ingredients

Rip Banana Honey Yogurt Castor oil

Procedure

Mash some Bananas as per your hair length. Mix 2-3 tea spoon of Honey and 3 tea spoon of Caster oil with it. Now add 1 table spoon of Yogurt with the mixture. Apply it all over your Hair from Root to the tip your hair. Apply this pack 1-2 times a week.

Benefit

Banana helps to repair damaged hair and helps in improving the hair texture. Yogurt helps to get a lustrous sheen to hair. Castor Oil nourishes and strengthen hair root. Honey helps in softening hair.
