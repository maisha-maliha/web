5 Easy Home Remedies to stop Hair fall and get strong Hair Root

By Maisha

Wherever you go, "hair fall" is a common problem among both Men and women. Women are facing either excess hair fall or excess hair thinning problem. Even Men are facing hair fall problem drastically. 50% Men of 50+ age are facing baldness or male pattern baldness. Balding is the number one reason behind men's hair fall.

One starts loosing hair due to various reasons. Aging, pregnancy, sickness,etc causes hair fall. But sometimes it is us who are responsible for our hair loss. Our lifestyle and carelessness leads to hair loss. We must keep our hair and scalp clean. Now here are some very easy tips to stop Hair fall and get strong hair root.

Before you go for any solution you must know whether you have anything serious to worry about or not. Check out my blog post 18 Main Causes of Hair fall .

1.Egg

Take 1 complete egg and mix it with 2/3 tea spoon of coconut oil and 2 spoon of honey very well in a small bowl. Now wash your hair with some water only. Then massage your scalp with the mixture using your fingers thoroughly.Apply rest of the mixture all over your hair. Keep it for 20 minutes to 1 hour. Then wash it with clean warm water. Do this for 3/4 times per week for 1 month. You are going to get a very smooth, conditioned strong hair.

This egg remedy is the best. It stops hairfall, makes your hair strong, smooth and also helps in hair growth. If you only do this and daily drink water properly, all your hair problems will be solved unless you have some hair disease.

2.Onion/Garlic/Ginger juice

Onion juice is the best solution for hair loss. Garlic or Ginger juice are also good for hair. So repeat it 2-3 times per week for 1/2 moths. Method-1:At first blend some onions/Garlic/Ginger in the blender and then apply them directly on you scalp. Keep it for 15 minutes. Method-2: Or you can extract juice from them and then massage the juice in your scalp and then apply in your hair. Keep it for 15 minutes. Method-3:If you want, you can add a tea spoon of coconut oil or honey with the juice and then apply it all over your hair and Keep it for 30 minutes.

Caution- Those who are allergic to onions avoid this. Sometimes certain medications such as aspirin can increase sensitivity to onions.

3.tea massage

Simply take 1 tea spoon of tea in 1 cup of water and heat it in the stove.When the tea water starts boiling take it out of the stove and cool it down. Then mix 2 tea spoon of lemon juice with it.Now wash you hair and then massage the tea mixture in you scalp and hair. Keep it for 15 minutes. Then wash it off. Repeat this 1-2 times a week for a few months.

4.Henna pastes

Henna pastes are good for your. They not only stops hair fall but also conditions your hair and gives a beautiful color to your hair. Try this paste once every week for 1 Month. You can either use a henna powder( Organic henna) or blended henna leaves. Mix tea water / 1 egg with henna thoroughly. Then Apply the paste all over your hair. After wards tie your hair and keep it for at least 30 minutes. Wash your hair properly with mild shampoo in warm water. Make sure you wash you hair properly or you will loose more hair because of dirty hair root.

5.Coconut/Olive/Castor oil

Last but not least. If you don't want any bad smell in your hair, all you can do is apply pure coconut oil or olive oil or Castor oil. Keep the pure coconut oil is in a bottle. Put the bottle inside a small cooking pot filled with water and then heat it in the stove. In this way we are going to heat the coconut oil.When the water starts boiling put the pot down. After a while pour coconut oil in small bowl according to need and cool it down according to tolerance. Then Apply the coconut oil in your scalp and hair. Make sure the oil is slightly hot but not too hot or cool. Just normally warm. Massage you hair for 5-7 minutes and keep it for an hour and then wash your hair with mild shampoo.

For Castor oil, Mix it with honey and massage your hair for 2-3 minutes.Keep it for 30 minutes.Then wash it properly with mild shampoo.Do it once per week. Regular application of only Castor oil will increase your hair length. As for olive oil, You can normally apply it and massage your scalp for 3-5 minutes and leave it over night. Apply 2 Times per week. It's good for hair and gets all the dirt out.



So these are very simple and easy ways to stop hair fall and build strong hair root. These are very good for hair conditioning and voluminous hair.
